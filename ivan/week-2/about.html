Vani Hypercars

1991

Vani Hypercars story is one of humble beginnings, dedication to a vision and an unyielding commitment to automotive perfection. Take a look at the timeline below to see where we’ve come from. The only story more exciting than where we’ve been is where we’re going in the future!

Like any startup, Vani Hypercars was not easy.
The reason was that the idea of introducing super cars to fans was actually born earlier ,
than the super cars themselves.

14 July 1996

After the first 5 difficult years, the first car that was introduced to our fans was Koenigsegg CC.
Driven in public for the first time by Rickard Rydell at an event at Anderstorp race track in 1996.
All were amazed by the Koenigsegg’s outstanding performance.
The concept worked. It was time to introduce Koenigsegg to prospective buyers.
